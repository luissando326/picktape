This matters more than your record

Gambling addiction is real.
So is the damage it causes.

Sports betting is more accessible than ever. Apps make it easy to place a bet in seconds, and the excitement of MMA makes it even harder to step back and think clearly. Problem gambling doesn't just hurt your wallet — it affects your relationships, your mental health, and your life.

PICKTAPE was built with this reality in mind. By tracking every pick honestly — including the losses you'd rather forget — you get a clear picture of your actual relationship with betting. That self-awareness is the first and most important step toward keeping it under control.

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, help is available right now. You don't have to figure it out alone.
